# Project Overview

## Purpose
Rebuild the windsanity-mini-splits.com website as a custom-coded, maintainable, and reusable template for small service businesses. The new site will improve flexibility, lower costs, and enable future template reuse.

## Business/Site Goals
- Generate qualified leads for mini-split installation and service
- Build credibility and trust with local customers
- Educate visitors about mini-splits and services
- Showcase completed projects and testimonials
- Support future blog content for SEO and education

## Primary Audience
- Homeowners and small business owners seeking mini-split solutions in the service area

## Primary Conversion Goal
- Capture leads via contact forms and calculator submissions

## Template Intent
- This project is also a reusable starter template for future small-business marketing sites, especially for contractors and service providers.
