# 10-content-examples.md

This document provides **example content blocks** that AI can use as reference when generating pages and sections for the Windsanity Mini-Splits website.

These examples demonstrate:

- tone
- structure
- length
- call-to-action patterns
- card layout patterns

The goal is **consistent page generation across the site**.

---

# Brand Voice & Tone

Voice should be:

- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Clear and educational
- Contractor/service-business friendly
- Confident but not overly technical

Messaging should emphasize:

- veteran-owned leadership
- experience
- reliable comfort solutions
- energy efficiency
- personalized installation

Example positioning:

> Windsanity Mini-Splits is a veteran-owned HVAC specialist providing efficient ductless heating and cooling solutions for homes and businesses.

# Value Proposition Section Example

## Why Choose Mini-Splits?

Mini-split HVAC systems provide an energy-efficient and flexible solution for heating and cooling individual spaces without traditional ductwork.

**Benefits include:**

- Lower energy usage
- Quiet operation
- Zoned temperature control
- Flexible installation for unique spaces

Mini-split systems are ideal for homes, garages, offices, workshops, and renovation projects where traditional HVAC systems are impractical.

# Content Length Guidance

20–40 words

20–50 words

80–150 words

**Blog excerpts**

20–40 words

**CTA banners**

10–25 words

# Content Generation Rules

When AI generates new content:

- Maintain a professional HVAC contractor tone
- Emphasize experience and reliability
- Avoid excessive technical jargon
- Include clear CTAs
- Keep paragraphs short and readable
